\noindent
Lotka-Volterra population\\
dynamics:
\\*[12pt]
\indent
\( \frac{dX_i}{dt} = r_i X_i + \sum_j a_{ij} X_i X_j
\)
\\*[12pt]
\noindent
``Interaction term'' $a_{ij}$ describes\\
the effect of population $j$ on\\
population $i$.
